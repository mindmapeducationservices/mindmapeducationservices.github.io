Who we are

About MindMap Education Services

MindMap Education Services is a professional Australian education services provider offering STEM tutoring, robotics learning, and academic mentoring for students from Year 7 through to university level.

Our services are delivered fully online, making expert academic support accessible to students across Melbourne, Brisbane, and all of Australia.

Our educator holds a PhD in mechatronics engineering, with extensive experience in robotics, STEM education, and research mentoring. We are passionate about helping students connect ideas and build genuine confidence in STEM.
